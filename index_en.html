About Us

An escape room is an interactive real-life escape game. Our escape rooms in the heart of Turku offer a novel and real game experience, where the goal of your group is to solve the game and escape the room before the hour is up.
The themes of our escape rooms change annually, and we have suitable experiences for the children as well.
The perfect package for spending an evening, a team-building event, a Finnish "Little Christmas," student events or just a revitalizing Sunday.

Are you up to the challenge??

Pakohuone Oy has a longstanding history of operating as a genuine Finnish escape room experience since 2022. Our escape rooms are the best. Really. Literally. I mean it. We can prove it. Mathematically. Be on the right side of science. Book a room.
With us, you'll find an expert personnel and an exciting escape room experience. The majority of our rooms are designed for people over the age of 16, but we also have options for younger participants.
