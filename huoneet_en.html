ESCAPE ROOMS

Our room themes change annually, and we have options for younger participants as well. Choose your own adventure from one of the four rooms below!





ROOM 1 - THE MAD SCIENTIST

Suggested age: PG-12

Escape time: 60 minutes

Language: English, Finnish

Jason Born, a crazy scientist fresh out of the University of Helsinki, wants to melt down the polar ice sheets by building a wack quantum nuclear bomb.
You are members of the SpecOps group A.M.M.A, and your mission is to stop Jason Born before he finishes the bomb. With the aid of a clever hint, you end up in the laid of the mad scientist, but of him there is no trace.
However, he seems to have left clues in his wake... Can you stop him before the fateful timer hits its last zero?





ROOM II - JACK THE RIPPER

Suggested age: PG-16

Escape time: 60 minutes

Language: English, Finnish

Victorian London, smog-choked streets and a scream in the night. Jack the Ripper is on the move, and the entire London sleeps uneasily.
Who is this Jack, and how can he move in the fog like a wraith?
The final solution to all this can be found only in his own territory, and your group has a limited amount of time to bring it all to light before Jack finds his next victim... or victims.





ROOM III - THE ROYAL POISONING

Suggested age: PG-7

Escape time: 60 minutes

Language: English, Finnish

Oh no! A king has been poisoned in Legolas City. See the middle ages with your own eyes, and find the person responsible for trying to poison the king's chalice.





ROOM IV - THE WITCH'S CURSE

Suggested age: PG-16

Escape time: 60 minutes

Language: English, Finnish, Old Norse

Your hiking group got lost in a forest which the map claimed was far smaller than it seemed to be.
Luckily, you found an abandoned hut to stay the night in, but a good night's rest was not to come: when the witching hour struck at midnight, everyone in the hut was awoken with an ominous chill of a curse taking hold.
You have only until the end of the witching hour to find a way to undo the curse, and the only help you can find seems to be a cryptic manuscript found lying in the hut...
